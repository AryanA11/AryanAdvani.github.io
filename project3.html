Photo Alphabet

Photoshop

Using a chex mix database, I created a chex mix alphabet mimicking photorealism. The chex mix were indivitually picked for each letter and layered in respect to the physics and balance of it. To add another aspect of photorealism, I added feathered shaodows that are apparent on each chex mix piece as well.
